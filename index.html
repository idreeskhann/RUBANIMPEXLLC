HUNTER ENGINEERING COMPANY

Hunter Engineering Company is a top manufacturer of automotive service equipment, specializing in wheel alignment and tire balancing. Known for its innovation and quality, Hunter delivers advanced solutions that enhance efficiency and safety for automotive professionals worldwide.

TEXA

Texa is a leading provider of innovative diagnostic solutions for the automotive, industrial, and marine sectors. Known for its cutting-edge technology, Texa helps technicians quickly and accurately diagnose and repair vehicles, enhancing performance and customer satisfaction.

DEA ITALIAN WORKLAB

DEA Italian Workshop Furniture specializes in high-quality, stylish furniture designed for workshops and creative spaces. Combining functionality with Italian craftsmanship, From workbenches to storage solutions, DEA focuses on creating environments that inspire productivity and creativity.

PIUSI

PIUSI is a leader in fluid handling innovation, specializing in pumps, meters, and accessories for the transfer and management of fuels and liquids. Known for quality and efficiency, their products enhance safety and reliability across various industries.

MIDTRONICS

Midtronics is a leader in vehicle electrification, tackling the challenges of new vehicles and battery technologies. With our proven expertise, we deliver innovative solutions that enhance performance and reliability, driving the future of transportation forward.

BHISHAMON

Bishamon specializes in high-quality material handling and logistics equipment, offering products like pallet jacks, lift tables, stackers, and scissor lifts. Their focus is on durability and efficiency, providing innovative solutions that enhance workplace safety and productivity across various industries.

REICHERT AMETEK

Reichert offers precision measurement solutions, including refractometers and microscopes, for automotive, laboratory, and medical applications.

SCHUMACHER ELECTRIC

Schumacher Electric is a top manufacturer of battery chargers, jump starters, and power management solutions, known for their reliability and innovation in automotive and industrial markets. They focus on quality and user-friendly designs to keep vehicles and equipment ready for use.

FIAC

FIAC specializes in high-quality compressed air solutions, including air compressors and accessories. Renowned for their innovation and reliability, FIAC serves diverse industries, delivering efficient and durable products that enhance productivity and performance.

ISTOBAL

Istobal is a leading manufacturer of car wash systems, offering automatic washes, self-service stations, and water recycling solutions. Known for innovation and sustainability, they provide effective cleaning technology for both commercial and retail markets.

SUPERTECH FUEL SAVER

SuperTech Fuel Saver is a fuel additive that enhances engine performance and improves fuel efficiency by reducing friction, cleaning injectors, and preventing deposits. It helps optimize fuel consumption and promotes smoother vehicle operation.
